About me

I am a senior researcher in the Research Unit at the Rockwool Foundation and a Research Fellow at IZA.

My primary research interests are in applied microeconomics, particularly labor and health economics. My recent work looks into the returns to medical treatments, particularly early in life, and the links between health and the labor market.
